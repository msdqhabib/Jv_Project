Frequently Asked Questions

1 What are the documents required for Joint Venture (JV) proposal?
  • Company profile and scope of projects completed and/or in progress
  • Company incorporation documents (MOA, AOA)
  • Audited statement of financial position for the last 5 years
  • Bank statement and FBR tax returns
  • Source of funds for the project
    • 2 What are the types of JV model?
  • Inventory Sharing
  • Net Profit Sharing
  • Gross Revenue Sharing
  • Concession Agreement
  • Rent/License Agreement
  • Hybrid Model
    • 3 What about financing Joint Ventures?
  • Initially 25% of project finance will be invested by JV Partner/Investor from its own resources before start of sale. In case of variance in forecasted sales the shortfall in financing will be arranged by JV Partner/Investor.
  • Pledge/mortgage of Project Land?
  • Project land will not be pledged as collateral by JV Partner/Investor for seeking loan from banks or finance parties.
    • 4 What assets can be put into Joint Ventures?
  • DHA will invest land and JV Partner/Investor will be responsible for financial, technical, sales, marketing etc.
    • 5 What assets can be put into Joint Ventures?
  • DHA will invest land and JV Partner/Investor will be responsible for financial, technical, sales, marketing etc.
    • 6 What will be the scope of joint ventures?
  • The joint ventures are open to all development/construction projects namely mixed used residential-cum-commercial high rise, hospitality, shopping malls, entertainment arenas, theme parks, community clubs, universities, hospital, schools etc
  • Project Profit: Preferred type of project profit will be in the form of inventory in development project.
  • Payment of Utility Bills: All utilities bills (electricity, gas, water etc) related to the project site will be paid by JV Partner/Investor.
